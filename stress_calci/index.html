Terminology:

  • σxx or εxx : Normal Stress/Strain in +x direction on +x plane
  • σyy or εyy : Normal Stress/Strain in +y direction on +y plane
  • τxy or εxy : Shear Stress/Strain in +y direction on +x plane or vice-versa
  • θi : Arbitrary angle of the plane on which you want stress/strain analysis
  • σ1 or ε1 and σ2 or ε2 : Principal Stress/Strain values for the given 2d problem
  • θp : Angle of the Principal plane
  • (σxx)i or (εxx)i : σxx or εxx values on the arbitrary plane
  • (σyy)i or (εyy)i : σyy or εyy values on the arbitrary plane
  • (τxy)i or (εxy)i : τxy or εxy values on the arbitrary plane
  • Stress and Strain values will be having same units that you mention...
  • Also type angles in degrees and the result of angles will be in degrees...
